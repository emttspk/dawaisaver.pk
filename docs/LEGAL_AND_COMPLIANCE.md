# Legal and Compliance

## Important Note

This document is an engineering planning artifact, not legal advice. Pakistan-specific healthcare, pharmacy, consumer protection, data privacy, and advertising requirements must be reviewed by qualified counsel before launch.

## Compliance Areas

- pharmacy licensing
- DRAP data usage and attribution
- medicine advertising rules
- consumer protection
- data privacy and retention
- prescription and bill document handling
- partner pharmacy agreements
- marketplace commissions
- warehouse storage and distribution

## Product Safety Position

DawaiSaver.pk should present medicine intelligence, price comparison, and alternative candidates. It must not tell users to change prescribed medicines without professional confirmation.

## Required Policies Before Public Launch

- privacy policy
- terms of service
- medical disclaimer
- data retention policy
- pharmacy partner terms
- content and recommendation policy
- user consent flow for uploaded documents

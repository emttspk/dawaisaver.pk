# Business Model

## Initial Value

DawaiSaver.pk creates value by helping users understand medicine costs, compare equivalent products, identify savings opportunities, and make more informed pharmacy purchases.

## Potential Revenue Streams

- pharmacy partner leads
- marketplace commission
- pharmacy analytics subscriptions
- manufacturer intelligence reports
- premium consumer reports
- enterprise data products, subject to legal review

## Non-Negotiables

- Do not sell personal prescription or bill data.
- Do not compromise recommendation integrity for paid placement.
- Clearly separate sponsored offers from intelligence results.
- Keep user trust as the primary asset.

## Key Metrics

- normalized medicine count
- price observations per city
- prescription savings generated
- bill savings generated
- user repeat rate
- verified pharmacy coverage
- review queue accuracy
